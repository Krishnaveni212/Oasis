Welcome to the vice city it is popular open-world action adventure video game relesed by Rockstar Games.
The game is set in the fictional city of Vice City, which is heavily inspired by 1980s Miami. Players assume the role of Tommy Vercetti, a former mobster who is seeking revenge and power in Vice City.
The game offers a vast and immersive world for players to explore, featuring a mix of urban areas, beaches, and rural locations.!
